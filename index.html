Campus Calculation Center

This Flask application was developed as part of a computer science class project, aiming to demonstrate practical web development skills and interactive tool creation. It features three distinct functionalities designed to handle specific types of calculations and data management:

  • Calculate Weekday: Determine the day of the week for any given day of the year.
  • Wedding Planning: Help couples calculate the number of buses needed for their wedding guests.
  • Jackalope Population: Estimate the population of jackalopes over a number of generations.
